# Resume Bullet Points - Kin App
## For Associate Product Manager Position at Duolingo

---

## Product Strategy & Vision

• **Designed and launched a mobile-first family wellness app** that increased user engagement through gamification mechanics (streaks, points, leaderboards) and community accountability features, resulting in a cohesive product experience that balances individual progress with family motivation

• **Architected a 5-step onboarding flow** that reduced friction for new users by consolidating profile setup, goal configuration, and family creation into an intuitive guided experience, improving activation rates and reducing drop-off during first-time use

• **Defined product requirements and user flows** for a faith-based fitness tracker integrating health data, AI verification, and social features, translating user needs into technical specifications for a cross-functional team

---

## User Engagement & Retention

• **Implemented streak-based gamification system** with visual progress indicators and dynamic encouragement messaging, driving daily active usage through psychological principles of consistency and achievement recognition

• **Designed family leaderboard and activity feed features** that leveraged social accountability to increase workout completion rates by creating competitive dynamics and celebrating milestones within family groups

• **Built AI-powered photo verification system** using OpenAI Vision API to validate workout completions, reducing manual verification overhead while maintaining trust and authenticity in user-reported activities

---

## Technical Product Management

• **Collaborated with engineering to integrate Apple HealthKit** for automatic step tracking, reducing user friction and improving data accuracy by eliminating manual entry while maintaining privacy compliance with iOS health data guidelines

• **Spearheaded development of real-time data synchronization** using Supabase backend, enabling live updates across family members' devices for leaderboards, activity feeds, and progress tracking without manual refresh

• **Managed product roadmap and feature prioritization** for MVP launch, balancing core functionality (authentication, task tracking, social features) with stretch goals (notifications, achievements) based on user value and technical complexity

---

## Data-Driven Decision Making

• **Designed analytics-ready database schema** with cached metrics (streaks, points, completion rates) to enable future A/B testing of gamification mechanics and personalization features, supporting data-informed product iterations

• **Created user preference system** allowing customizable goals (workout frequency, step targets) and reminder settings, enabling personalized experiences that adapt to individual user needs and family dynamics

---

## Cross-Functional Collaboration

• **Led end-to-end product development** from concept to implementation, working closely with design, engineering, and backend teams to deliver a React Native mobile app with full authentication, onboarding, and social features

• **Defined and documented API integrations** for OpenAI Vision, Supabase storage, and HealthKit, ensuring seamless data flow and error handling across external services while maintaining product reliability

• **Established product requirements and user stories** for family invite system, task verification workflow, and notification architecture, translating complex social dynamics into clear technical specifications

---

## User Experience & Design

• **Crafted cohesive design system** with intentional color palette and typography that reinforced brand identity (grounded, peaceful, purposeful), creating a calming user experience that differentiated the product in the wellness app market

• **Designed intuitive three-tab navigation structure** (Home, Family, Profile) that prioritized daily engagement while maintaining easy access to social features and settings, optimizing for both new and returning user needs

• **Built responsive UI components** with accessibility considerations, ensuring consistent experience across iOS devices with proper error states, loading indicators, and empty states that guide users through key actions

---

## Key Metrics & Impact

• **Reduced onboarding completion time** by consolidating user setup into a single flow with clear progress indicators, improving user activation and reducing time-to-value

• **Enabled family accountability features** that increased social engagement through shared goals, leaderboards, and activity feeds, creating network effects that improve retention for all family members

• **Implemented scalable data architecture** supporting real-time updates and future growth, with optimized database schema that balances query performance with data integrity for user streaks, points, and activity history
